About Patrick Byrne

Monday, 5 September 2011

Hi, there. I'm Patrick Byrne, but I guess you knew that already.

I make things, mostly websites and related tools. Right now, I work for a great company in Minneapolis making collaborative tools for organizations. In past lives, I worked on internal web tools for a Fortune 500 company, search engines for one of its subsidiaries, and much earlier managed a quite large web store.

I'm passionate about how technology can make our lives better (not just more complex), which is why you'll generally find me using Apple products in my day-to-day life. I'd mention that I love good movies, good music, and believe that we're in a second golden age of television, but I can't figure out how to not sound incredibly generic in saying that.

Probably the best way to find out more about me is to read what I tweet (or far less frequntly, write), take a look at my code, or get a hold of me.
